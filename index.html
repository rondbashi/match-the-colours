How to play

  1. 1

    Match the colours

    Connect two same-coloured blocks by sliding them horizontally or vertically.

  2. 2

    Slide rows & columns

    Click a block and it will show the directions you can slide it. You can't slide if connected blocks are in the way.

  3. 3

    Split with the scissors

    If you're stuck, click the connected blocks and you can split them.

  4. 4

    Connect them all to win

    Connect all the pairs to win! Beat your best time, or try hard mode!
